# Burger-shop

A vitrual burger shop that allows users to order a different burgers with different sides. The shop simultaneously updates the receipt as the users update their orders.


## Features:
### Burger types:
- Normal Burger:
  - Choose type of bread
  - Choose 1 meat
  - Choose 4 sides
- Healthy Burger:
  - Whole Wheat bread
  - Choose 1 meat
  - Choose 4 sides
- Deluxe Burger:
  - Choose type of bread
  - Choose 1 meat
  - Choose 6 sides
  - Chips and fries
